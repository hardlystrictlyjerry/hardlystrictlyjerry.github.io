This appearance at Honolulu Dead Night occurred a week after the Floods of March 2026, which devastated many farms on the North Shore. Matt had the great idea of making it a benefit, and we raised over $2,000 for flood relief. Epic win! 

First Set: Cold Rain and Snow, Big River, Deep Elem Blues, West LA Fadeaway, Evangeline, Dear Mr. Fantasy, Brown-eyed Women, Cassidy, Casey Jones.

Second Set: Jack Straw, Alabama Getaway, After Midnight, Midnight Moonlight, Deal, Crazy Fingers, Women are smarter, Good Lovin', Shakedown Street

Encore: Franklin's Tower, Going Down the Road Feelin Bad, And We Bid You Goodnight
