Both literally and figuratively, it was a hot Friday night at the Garden. There was World Cup on the TV all day long, and Hardly Strictly Jerry took over for the night. Our faithful fans showed up once again and made it an epic dance party. Matt arranged a special guest appearance by [Varun Jhunhjunwalla](https://www.instagram.com/varunjhunjhunwalla) which was the cherry on top of the jam band cake.

First Set: Samson and Delilah, Big River, Sugaree, No Raindrops, Eyes of the World, Loving Cup, Stagger Lee, Playing in the Band, Arcadia.

Second Set: Bertha, Good Lovin, Down By The River, The Music Never Stopped, Sand, West LA Fadeaway, Scarlet Begonias<sup>*</sup>, Fire On The Mountain<sup>*</sup>, Everything's Right

Encore: Sugar Magnolia<sup>*</sup>

<sup>*</sup> featuring Varun Jhunjhunwalla
